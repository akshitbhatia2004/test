Progressive Assessment Tool for Children With C.P. (P.A.T.-C.W.C.P.)

Assessment Score Analysis

Instructions:

  • If the child scores **0-20%**, they are classified under the **Profound** category.
  • If the child scores **20-40%**, they are classified under the **Severe** category.
  • If the child scores **40-60%**, they are classified under the **Moderate** category.
  • If the child scores **60-80%**, they are classified under the **Mild** category.
  • If the child scores **80-100%**, they are classified as **Normal**.
