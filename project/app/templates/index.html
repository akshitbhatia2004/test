Welcome to P.A.T.-C.W.C.P.

This assessment tool is designed to evaluate children with Cerebral Palsy (C.P.) in various activity areas.

Child Activities Assessed

  • Motor Skills: Sitting, walking, holding objects, balancing.
  • Communication: Responding to name, following instructions, speaking.
  • Social Interaction: Recognizing family, interacting with peers.
  • Daily Living Skills: Dressing, eating, toilet use.
  • Cognitive Abilities: Identifying shapes/colors, understanding routines.
